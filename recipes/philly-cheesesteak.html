Philly Cheesesteak Sloppy Joes

Description

Take the best of two classic sandwiches—sloppy joes and Philly cheesesteaks—to make these cheesy, meaty, and saucy cheesesteak sloppy joes!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 small white onion, chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, seeded and chopped
  • 1/2 cup beef stock
  • 1/4 cup ketchup
  • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce

Steps

  1. Cook the vegetables
  2. Cook the beef
  3. Make it saucy
  4. Assemble the sloppy joes

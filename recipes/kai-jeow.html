Kai Jeow (Thai Omelet)

Description

Kai jeow is a classic Thai omelet with the perfect balance of salty-sweet-hot flavors. Serve it simply, with rice and raw vegetables, for a quick and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons fish sauce
  • 1/4 teaspoon brown sugar
  • 1 scallion, minced
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 to 3 tablespoons vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Whisk the eggs
  2. Heat the oil smoking hot
  3. Pour the eggs into the hot skillet and cook
  4. Serve
